## **Red Team (Attackers)**

To simulate attacks and test the organization’s defenses to identify and address vulnerabilities.

## **Blue Team (Defenders)**

To protect and defend the organization’s assets through robust security measures and incident response.

## **Yellow Team (Builders)**

To design and develop secure systems and applications, incorporating security from the beginning.

## **White Team (Oversight)**

To oversee and coordinate all teams, managing strategy, compliance, and overall security posture.

## **Black Team (Architekts)**

The master architects who orchestrate and unify all security elements, crafting comprehensive strategies from inception to execution.
