This is my attempt to map out the web dev experience to make it easier for newcomers to get started and seniors to look up information or forgotten protocols or design patterns they might want to use.

I'll try to cover everything from getting started with html to robust design system principles and list out as many usefull websites as I know to always expands your knowledge set.

> "It's okay to now know the subject 100% and that perfection isn't needed. Try it, fail fast and iterate - you don't need to spend ages reading up on things and procrastinating and don't let feart of failing because 'you don't know enough' hold you back"
