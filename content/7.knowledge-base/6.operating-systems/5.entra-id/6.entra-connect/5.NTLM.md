## New Technology LAN Manager

NTLM is a legacy authentication protocol using a challenge/response mechanism to verify users and computers. It supports LAN Manager and NTLM versions 1 and 2, and authenticates against a domain controller or local account database.

## When it's used

* Workgroup systems
* Local logon on non-domain controllers
* Legacy or non-Microsoft apps
* When Kerberos isn't available

::note
Kerberos is preferred in AD environments, but NTLM is still needed in some cases. Tools and settings are available to help audit and reduce NTLM usage in your environment.
::
