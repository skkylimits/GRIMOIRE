## Medical Application: Diabetes

> phillippi2

I'm actually developing a resberry Pi to bridge a gap in my diabetes. It will read my blood sugars from a Continuous Glucose Monitor(CGM), process it and develop a curve. If my sugars are high, it uses that curve to develop a temporary basal rate to tell my insulin pump. As my sugars come down, it reevaluates the basal rate and tells the pump to lower the rate, if it need to.

If my sugars are too low, it will lower, or even stop, pumps injection rate. It will put the pump on Standbye. If the pump can't support that, a temporary basal rate of 0 is set, until it needs to be turned back on.

**edit:** The computer part of this is complete, on my end. I am currently dveloping a glucose monitour out of an Arduino Uno, because continued CGM support is not guaranteed.S
